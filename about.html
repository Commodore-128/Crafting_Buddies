Eva J.

Here is a little about me.

Eva is a talented artist with a desire to help kids of all ages become more creative. She enjoys helping people unlock their creative potential through the use of many different artistic mediums.

Clay, acrylic, wood, paper, oil, and wax. Anything can be used to create a masterpiece. Eva believes that challenging people to use new artistic mediums is the key to growing their artistic ability.
